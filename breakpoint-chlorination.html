What's Happening

Raw water contains ammonia (NH₃) and reducing agents (Fe²⁺, Mn²⁺, H₂S).

Click the button to add the first dose of chlorine and perform the DPD test.

DPD Test Procedure

1. Add chlorine to water sample
2. Add DPD indicator + KI → water turns red/pink
3. Titrate with FAS until red colour disappears
4. FAS consumed = chlorine residual
