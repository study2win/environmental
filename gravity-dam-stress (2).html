Dam Parameters

Calculated Results

p = (ΣV / B) × (1 ± 6e/B)
Where: ΣV = total vertical force, B = base width, e = eccentricity of resultant from center of base.
p_toe (max) uses +, p_heel (min) uses −. Tension occurs when e > B/6 (resultant outside middle third).
Notation: H' = dam height, H = head water (upstream) level, h = tail water (downstream) level, P_H = upstream water pressure, P_h = tail water pressure.
Note: The weight of the tail water standing above the sloped downstream face of the dam body has been ignored in these calculations.
